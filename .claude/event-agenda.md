# Standard CRM Meeting Agenda

| Topic                          | Time    |
|-------------------------------|---------|
| Arrivals & Check-In Go-Around | 10 min  |
| Agenda Review                 | 5 min   |
| Community Assembly            | 45 min  |
| Break                         | 10 min  |
| Member's Council              | 60 min  |
| Transition                    | 5 min   |
| Working Session               | 30 min  |
| Closing Circle                | 15 min  |

**Community Assembly** — public-facing, open to all. Typically a presentation, working group
update, demo, or facilitated discussion on a topic of broad community interest.

**Member's Council** — organizational body. Working group reports, proposals, decisions.
Friends may observe; only Members/Stewards vote.

**Working Session** — freeform breakout for Members/Stewards. Main space stays open for
socializing, connecting with newcomers, or exploring the technologies we use.

**Closing Circle** — brief (10–15 min), reunites the full group. Announcements, appreciations,
reflections. Surfaces anything from the Working Session breakout.
