## 🐍 Community Assembly: Rattlesnake Node Build

*Presented by Alex*

Alex designed the [Rattlesnake node](https://www.printables.com/model/1385495-rattlesnake-meshtastic-tree-node) as a rugged, deployable LoRa node built for real-world outdoor use. July's Community Assembly is a group build: Alex will bring the components, and attendees will assemble several units together.

These won't just be prototypes — they're the next batch of CRM's live infrastructure nodes, destined for deployment across the Capital Region. Units will be custom-printed with CRM branding, part of the broader effort to make our equipment identifiable in the field.

Building together is also a great way to understand the hardware from the ground up. Whether you've deployed nodes before or you're just getting started, this is a hands-on session you can contribute to.

**Have your own design?** Bring it. July is also an informal hardware show-and-tell — prototypes, experiments, and works-in-progress are all welcome. Show the group what you're building.
