## 📡 Reticulum: The General-Purpose Mesh

*Presented by Adam*

Reticulum brings together radio, mesh, and alternative network technologies to build a resilient general-purpose network. And you already have the technology to begin experimenting with it today.

Join the discussion and learn how Reticulum works, what sets it apart from other popular standards like Meshtastic and MeshCore, and how to get started using it.

No prior experience required — if you've got a LoRa radio or a Raspberry Pi gathering dust, this one's for you.
